# Rust-map-hider


## How to load script

---> open OBS studio

-> at the top of your screen, click the button tools.

<img width="1301" height="939" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/968cf6dc-f652-4b3d-af97-71a641319172" />


---> then click on scripts.

<img width="205" height="202" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/804a68f5-52bd-42ab-ab8a-0945336d95a7" />


---> After that click the + to add a new script.

<img width="777" height="522" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a9099274-1fe9-4430-a6ed-63b6f137ab97" />


---> select the file and open it.

<img width="786" height="439" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac8cf9e2-5a73-4db8-8f4c-39d0b355d8ee" />

---> Enter the names of your sources in both horizontal and vertical canvasses.

## To add the Keybind.

---> Open your settings in OBS studio

<img width="1308" height="939" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2ebbf2c6-0208-4890-9678-ec739a07fe7f" />


---> go over to the hotkey section in the sidebar.

<img width="978" height="761" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff56c9bb-2caf-4a4c-8be1-0da8dad34457" />


---> when you scroll down you should see a keybind called "Show Map Cover (Hold G)" and "Toggle Map Cover".

<img width="986" height="764" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/24e8a3ed-9d7f-47a8-ba8a-d2278e300487" />


---> Enter your preferred keys and your all set.
